Department of Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering Department is established in 1994. The department continuously strives to comply with the emerging technologies in the area of Computer. The department runs undergraduate and post graduate program. Computer department is highly proud of having qualified, dedicated and experienced staff. The staff attempts usage of various methodologies to enhance the understanding of the course to the students in the department. Besides conventional methods of teaching like lectures and tutorials, the staff encourages students to learn concepts of the course through workshops, group discussions, group projects and quiz etc. the faculty in the department is also involved in research in their specialized areas and department supports faculty to expertise the subject. The department started with an initial intake of 40 students in UG program and during the course of time, now it has been enhanced to 120. In 2011, the department started a PG program with an intake of 18 students and research center with intake of 10 student has also started in the department since 2015-16. The department is headed by Rajendra D. Gawali . His area of interest includes Database, Data Mining and information retrieval. The Head of department has rich experience of working at various level through his academic and industrial career.

Our Vision

The vision of the Computer Department is to create Computer Engineers who are technically sound and socially conscious and able to excel in multidisciplinary fields in global environment.

Our Mission

  • To impart professional knowledge through innovative teaching methodologies
  • To inculcate leadership qualities and entrepreneur skills with social and ethical values for technological development of the Nation.
  • To support technology transformation for societal and environmental benefits
  • To promote the skills for futuristic research practices.
